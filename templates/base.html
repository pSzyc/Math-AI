Math Sam

AI trained to understand basic math

Chatbot Sam was taught to perform basic mathematical operation on integers. Check it on your own! Open the chat by clicking on the icon below and greet him!

Behind Sam there are two feed-forward neural networks. First one, given user input, decides which type of predefined response to use and the second one tries to evaluate expression converted to a vector

Equations which Sam understand are those consisting of two values and an operator, for instance 21 + 37. Remember than in train set Sam saw only numbers lower than 100!
